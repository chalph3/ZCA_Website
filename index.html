RECREATIONAL CHEER

Perfect for preparing for your favorite competition school team. ZCA strives to keep your child with up to date coaching and new innovative cheer styles. Offering cheer for all ages and skill levels. We compete at local and out-of-state competitions with the intent to receive a qualifying bid to the Quest or UCA Nationals at Walt Disney World Resort in Florida. Competition season is generally set to begin in November and last until March. (Practices average 2-3 days a week)

ALL-STAR CHEER

Our all-star cheer program is a fast pace sport that continues to expand each year. We will prepare to compete at least 4-5 competitions each year with the possibility of 1-2 out of state competitions. Competition season is generally set to begin in November and last until April. Some competitions may have a duration of 2 days. (Practices average 2-3 days a week)

CHEER & TUMBLING CLASSES

For those interested in taking a tumbling or cheer class, we offer classes in each area to meet your needs with a variety of levels ranging from ages 2 on.
TUMBLE TOTS: AGES 3 & UNDER
CHEER IN TRAINING: AGES 10 & UNDER
CHEER PREP: AGES 10 & UP
TUMBLING: BEGINNER, INTERMEDIATE, AND ADVANCED
